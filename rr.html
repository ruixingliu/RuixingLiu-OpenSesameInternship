Question 3

What excites me about AI in learning and work

AI as a partner for better judgment and safer habits

What excites me most about AI is its ability to notice patterns humans miss while leaving judgment and accountability with people. In the context of OpenSesame, this means using AI to make both learning and GRC work more focused, relevant, and proactive.

For learning, AI can help turn compliance training from a one size experience into something adaptive and contextual:

  • highlighting which topics users tend to miss in phishing simulations and recommending targeted refreshers;
  • tailoring examples to the learner‚Äôs role, risk exposure, and language, so content feels less generic and more actionable;
  • generating draft micro lessons that reflect new threats or regulatory changes, which humans can then refine.

For the Security and Compliance team, AI can support audit readiness and risk monitoring: mapping controls to evidence, suggesting missing documentation, summarizing vendor responses, or flagging unusual trends in vulnerability findings. This does not replace careful review, but it gives the team better visibility and more time for higher level questions.

I am excited to work in a place where AI is used both to improve how people learn and to strengthen the security program itself. I would bring curiosity about new tools, caution about data protection and bias, and a strong interest in helping design AI assisted workflows that are transparent, well documented, and aligned with OpenSesame‚Äôs values.
